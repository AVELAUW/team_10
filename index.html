About WFC


Founded: March 20, 1930, by JULIAN, XIN, AWEIS, AND ABDUL in North Corbin, Kentucky

First Franchise: Opened in Salt Lake County, Utah, in 1952. Founder: Harland Sanders, also known as "Colonel Sanders," who became a prominent figure in American cultural history. We were all just a group of regular college kids and we all had one thing in common... We all liked fried chicken!!!. But we just couldn't find the right place to find the best fried chicken, so we all decided to make our own fried chicken with the right deals for Crispy, Juicy, Tender Flavour, We created WFC! Acquisition: Sold to a group of investors led by John Y. Brown Jr. and Jack C. Massey in 1964. Parent Company: Yum! Brands, which also owns Pizza Hut and Taco Bell.

Popular Items: Fried chicken (Original Recipe, Extra Crispy), chicken sandwiches, wraps, French fries, soft drinks, milkshakes, salads, desserts, and breakfast items. Nutrition: WFC provides a full nutrition guide for its menu items, detailing calories, fats, sugars, and other dietary information
